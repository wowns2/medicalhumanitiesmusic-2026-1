# Rain Man

The work I selected is the film *Rain Man*. It tells the story of Raymond, a man with autism spectrum disorder, and his relationship with his brother, portraying the condition in a realistic way. One of the key musical pieces is “Leaving Wallbrook / On the Road,” composed by Hans Zimmer. The music features repetitive rhythms and distinctive electronic sounds, reflecting Raymond’s structured and pattern-oriented thinking. The film follows the brothers’ journey as they gradually come to understand each other. The music represents the condition through its steady repetition and structured patterns. At the same time, its warm tones highlight Raymond’s humanity, presenting the disorder not just as a limitation but as a different way of experiencing the world.
