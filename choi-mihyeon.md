# The Theory of Everything

*The Story of Everything* (2014) and Arrival of the Birds (composed by Johan Johansson, 1969-2018) Physicist Stephen Hawking deals with the process of becoming paralyzed by Lou Gehrig's disease (ALS) and intellectual exploration that never stopped. In contrast to the desperate situation in which the body gradually becomes paralyzed, the music uses a very light and soaring melody. This contrasts the free intelligence and spirit of the protagonist, who depicts a disorder trapped in a prison called the body but goes beyond its limits and walks through space. This is an example of how music was used as a device to brilliantly illuminate human will, not define physical disabilities as sadness.
