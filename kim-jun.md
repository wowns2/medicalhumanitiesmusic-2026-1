# BlackSwan

The work I selected is Black Swan. This film depicts the psychological pressure and mental breakdown of a ballerina named Nina as she strives for perfection in her performance. The main music used in this work is “Swan Lake Theme” composed by Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky (1840–1893). This classical ballet music expresses tension and extreme emotions and represents the duality of purity and darkness (white swan vs.black swan). Nina is cast as the lead in Swan Lake and pushes herself to achieve perfection.As the pressure intensifies, she develops obsessive behaviors and experiences hallucinations,gradually losing touch with reality. In the end,she achieves perfection, but at a devastating cost.In this film, music plays a key role in portraying the protagonist’s psychological collapse. The “Swan Lake” theme begins as elegant and controlled but gradually becomes more intense and distorted,reflecting Nina’s growing anxiety and obsession. The changes in rhythm and dynamics effectively mirror her deteriorating mental state and enhance the emotional impact of the story.
