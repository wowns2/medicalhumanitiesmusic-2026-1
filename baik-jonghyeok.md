# joker

In the film *Joker*(2019), music serves as a device to starkly contrast the protagonist's mental breakdown with his sense of liberation. In particular, the heavy, slow cello melody (Hildur Guðnadóttir's score) represents the depression and deep inner sorrow Arthur experiences, whereas the exciting beat of 'Rock and Roll Part 2' in the staircase scene paradoxically glorifies his mental rampage. When he, who had been suffering from pathological laughter, no longer suppresses his laughter and embraces the madness itself, the music shifts from a tragic funeral march to an upbeat celebratory anthem, effectively visualizing the chilling process of the illness transforming into criminal charisma.
