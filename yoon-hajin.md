# Rent

The musical *Rent* (1996) is a reinterpretation of Jacomo Puccini's opera La Bohème into modern New York. The work deals with the lives of young artists living in East Village, New York, and shows the love, poverty, illness, and death of HIV/AIDS patients Angel and Roger, and their friends. In particular, Ainjeol died of AIDS complications and became an important turning point in the work.
