# shine

In *Shine* (1996), directed by Scott Hicks, is based on the true story of Australian pianist David Helfgott. The central piece of music in the film is the Piano Concerto No. 3 in D minor, Op. 30, composed by Sergei Rachmaninoff (April 1, 1873 – March 28, 1943), an instrumental concerto with no lyrics. The film follows David, a child prodigy raised under his father's oppressive control, who suffers a catastrophic mental breakdown immediately after performing the concerto at the Royal College of Music in London. Subsequently diagnosed with schizophrenia, he is institutionalized for years before eventually returning to the concert stage through the healing power of music and love.
